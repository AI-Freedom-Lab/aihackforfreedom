What is AI Hack for Freedom?

An intensive weekend hackathon where developers and innovators build solutions that advance freedom, privacy, and decentralized technology.

Get paired with real activists solving real problems. Every team is led by an activist "captain" with a genuine need for technology solutions.

Why Participate?

  • Build technology that creates real-world impact
  • Work alongside activists on meaningful problems
  • Connect with the freedom tech community
  • Compete for prizes and recognition
